Badminton Skills Video Tutorials

BEGINNERs:

  • Grip

    Learn the basic grips, including the forehand grip for standard shots and the backhand grip for more advanced plays. Proper gripping technique enhances control and power..

  • Footwork

    Develop agility and movement by practicing footwork drills. Efficient footwork allows you to reach the shuttlecock quickly and maintain balance during rallies.

  • Smashing

    Work on your smash to deliver powerful, aggressive shots that aim to win points outright. Timing and technique are crucial for an effective smash.

INTERMEDIATE:

  • Backhand shots

    Work on improving your backhand clear, backhand drive, and backhand drop shot. The key is to maintain proper grip and wrist movement for better control.

  • Defensive shots

    Improve your ability to handle smashes and powerful shots from your opponent. Work on defensive clears and lifts to keep the shuttlecock in play and regain control of the rally.

  • Net play

    Develop your skills at the net, including net shots, net kills, and tight net play. Practice quick reactions and precise placement to make it difficult for your opponent to return.

ADVANCED:

  • Dynamic shot combination

    Learn to seamlessly combine different shots within a rally. For example, use a high clear followed by a sudden drop shot or a fast smash followed by a deceptive flick. This keeps your opponent off-balance and guessing.
