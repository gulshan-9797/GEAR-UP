Self Defence Video Tutorials

BEGINNERs:

  • Palm Strikes

    Use the heel of your palm to strike an attacker’s nose or chin. Palm strikes are important because they allow you to deliver powerful blows without risking injury to your hand.

  • Escape Techniques

    Escape techniques are crucial because they provide quick and effective ways to break free from an attacker and ensure your safety.

  • Using Pressure Points

    Using pressure points is important because it can quickly incapacitate an attacker, giving you a chance to escape.

INTERMEDIATE:

  • Joint Locks and Manipulations

    Joint locks and manipulations are vital in martial arts for effectively controlling and neutralizing opponents through biomechanical leverage.

  • Ground Defense

    Develop skills to defend yourself on the ground, including escapes, sweeps, and submissions.

  • Weapon Disarmament

    Learn techniques to safely disarm an attacker wielding a knife, gun, or other weapon.Weapon disarmament is crucial for self-defense as it enables you to neutralize threats and prevent harm by safely removing an attacker’s weapon.

ADVANCED:

  • Multiple Attacker Defense

    Multiple attacker defense is crucial as it equips you with strategies and techniques to effectively handle and survive situations involving more than one assailant, significantly increasing your chances of staying safe.

  • Improvised Weapons

    Improvised weapons are crucial for self-defense as they enable you to use everyday objects to protect yourself when traditional weapons are unavailable

  • Chokeholds and Strangles

    Chokeholds and strangles are crucial in combat sports and self-defense for quickly incapacitating an opponent by restricting airflow or blood flow to the brain.
